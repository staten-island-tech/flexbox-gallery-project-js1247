Halloween Candies

Dum Dums

Dum Dums are small, round, and brightly colored lollipops that come in a variety of flavors.

Hershey's

Hershey's is a rectangular chocolate bar that can be easily broken into smaller pieces.

KitKat

KitKat consists of layers of crisp wafers that are separated by thin layers of flavored filling, all of which are then coated in milk chocolate.

M&M's

M&M's consist of small, round pieces of milk chocolate that are coated with a colorful candy shell.

Nerds

Nerds candy is known for its tiny, crunchy nuggets bursting with sweet and tangy flavors.

Reese's Cups

Reese's chocolate cup is filled with creamy peanut butter filling, creating a delicious combination of sweet and salty flavors.

Skittles

Skittles are brightly colored, fruit-flavored chewy candies that come in a variety of fruit flavors.

Snickers

Snickers consists of layers of nougat topped with caramel and peanuts, all covered in milk chocolate.

Sour Patch Kids

Sour Patch Kids are chewy, fruit-flavored candies that are known for their distinctive sour and sweet taste and come in a variety of fruit flavors.

Starbursts

Starburst candies are chewy, fruit-flavored sweets with bright colors and intense fruit flavors.

Tootsie Roll

Tootsie Roll are chewy, chocolate-flavored taffy-like texture, often wrapped in a brown paper wrapper.

Twix

Twix consists of a biscuit base topped with a layer of caramel and coated in milk chocolate.
